Health and Fitness Expo:  Vendor Information

The Anchorage Running Club’s Anchorage RunFest is one of Alaska’s most popular running events and attracts runners from all over the world. The event features a 49K, marathon, marathon walk, marathon relay, half marathon, 5K, mile, and kids’ 2K.


Location and Date

Location: Dena'ina Center, 600 West Seventh Avenue
Date: Friday, August 19, 2016 from 3 to 7 PM. and Saturday, August 20, 2016 from 9 AM to 4 PM.


Purpose

The ARF Health and Fitness Expo is a fun and informative event focusing on sports medicine, innovative fitness clothing, shoes, training methods and other fitness related products. Everyone is welcome to attend the free programs and lectures.


Targeted Audience

Promote your business in front of a targeted audience of fitness enthusiasts, walkers, joggers and both recreational and elite runners from all over the country.


Maintain Maximum Exposure

Participants in every ARF event must pick up their race packet from the expo so your business will gain exposure in front of all race participants, their families, and the general public. Admission is free and the race draws more than 3,000 people each year.


Advanced Promotion

You will get advanced promotion through the ARF website as well as radio spots and printed materials.


Booth Rental Rates

  • Standard 10'x10' -- $250
  • Double 20'x20' -- $450
  • Standard Corner -- $300
  • Non-profit -- $100
  • Swag Bag materials -- $50

Included in Booth Rental

  • 10' high backdrop
  • 3' high side skirts
  • one 6' skirted table
  • 2 chairs and wastebasket

Additional services such as electrical, AV, etc. are available from the show decorator.


Exhibitor Information and Booth Reservations

Download the Expo Information Sheet, the ARF Expo Exhibitor Agreement, and the ARF Swag Bag Application for the 2015 Expo. 2016 information will be posted as soon as it is available.
