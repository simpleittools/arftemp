Health and Fitness Expo

Location and Date

Location: Dena'ina Center, 600 West Seventh Avenue
Date: Friday, August 19, 2016 from 3 to 7 PM. and Saturday, August 20, 2016 from 9 AM to 4 PM.


Expo Clinics

Our pre-race clinics at the Health and Fitness Expo are FREE!
Stop by, learn something new, and enjoy!

2016 Clinics have not been determined yet. To get an idea though, check out the clinics we offered in 2015.



The "Empower" Hour
Tara Lampert discusses safety tips and self-defense products in addition to a discussion about becoming more self-aware of your surroundings.

Date: Friday, August 14 – 5:30 PM

Tara Lampert with Damsel in Defense will present safety tips for when you are out running and ways that you can defend yourself. Tara will discuss how to be more aware of your surroundings and will demonstrate how Damsel in Defense’s products can be used to defend yourself.  The mission of Damsel in Defense is to Equip, Empower, and Educate.

About Tara Lampert:
Tara teaches Women's Studies at the University of Alaska; therefore, women's topics are very important to her. Tara recently finished teaching a course on women and incarceration and she is currently teaching a course on human trafficking. Violence and trauma is at the core of both subjects.  She hopes to make a difference in the community with Damsel in Defense.


Are You Power Hungry?
Nutrition for power and endurance: power foods, what’s out there on the race-course (and how best to use it) and post race nutrition with Heidi Beer.

Date: Saturday, August 15 - 10:00 AM

Take advantage of the nutrition education expertise of health, wellness and fitness professional and long-time endurance athlete Heidi Beer. Coach Heidi’s presentation will give you the right edge to make your next race your personal best. Are you just hungry or power hungry?

About Heidi Beer:
Heidi Beer has worked as a professional in the Health, Wellness and Fitness field for over 20 years. She is known for her multi-sport training, running clinics, and nutrition coaching. She holds numerous certifications and credentials in fitness, kinesiology, and the biomechanics of running as well as wellness coaching and sports nutrition. She has counseled and lectured on whole food and whole nutrition and the vegetarian and vegan sports diets. She has been vegan raising her family of four children as vegans for over 25 years.

Heidi has competed in over 85 Triathlons, and over 100 footraces from 1 mile to 100K. She is currently writing a book about running with her long time running partner Professor Toby Widdicombe. The title of the book is “The Religion of Running, an Examination of Human Devotion, Transformation and Inspiration.”


Breathing Your Way to a Stronger Core
A hands-on workshop with Studio One Pilates Training Director, Certified Advanced Rolfer® Ana Kokaurova.

Date: Saturday, August 15 - 11:00 AM

Ana Kokaurova explains the science and practical application of how to activate your deep core reflexes. Learn how taking 5 minutes to breathe before your run can help you to engage your stabilizing muscles and put your core on autopilot.

About Ana Kokaurova:
Ana is the training director and general manager at Studio One Pilates. She currently teaches barre, whole body boot camp, reformer and mat classes and pre and post natal pilates. She is a certified advanced rolfer® and STOTT pilates certified. Ana apprenticed at BalletMet in Columbus, Ohio, was a Joffrey Ballet student through New School University (NY), attended the SUNY Purchase Ballet Conservatory and was a student at Anchorage Classical Ballet Academy.
