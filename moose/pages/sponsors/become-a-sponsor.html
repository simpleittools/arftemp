About Us

The Anchorage RunFest (ARF) has been a staple event in the Anchorage community since 2008. Over the past few years, the race has evolved from a small running event into a world‐class marathon affair attracting runners from across the globe. ARF has become a much‐loved event in the Anchorage community and it is thanks to community member support that we have been able to realize our mission of encouraging a healthy lifestyle through running and exercise.

The Anchorage Running Club is a 501(c)(3) not‐for‐profit organization. Funds raised by the Anchorage RunFest (ARF) help support the club's mission to promote a healthy lifestyle through running and exercise. ARF is the main fundraiser for the ARC. The strength of the ARC and the success of ARF rely on the support and partnership of the Anchorage community.


Where Some of Our Funds Went in 2014

  • College scholarships
  • Local high school clubs and sports teams
  • Free ARC member runs at the Alaska Sports Dome
  • Kids' 2K - free registration

Community Support Has Made ARF a Success!

  • 98.5% of participants surveyed said they would recommend ARF to a friend.
  • 68.2% of participants surveyed said they planned on participating in ARF again.

Demographics

  • 65% of participants are women.
  • 35% of participants are men.
  • The median age of women is 37.
  • The median age of men is 34.
  • 86% of participants were from Alaska. Of those, 72% were from the Anchorage area.

Sponsorship Opportunities

We are always appreciative of sponsor support to help defray some of the costs associated with putting on this event and to enable us to keep registration fees low. These fees do not cover the entire cost of putting on a race of this size. For 2015, the estimated costs were $210,000.

We welcome sponsorship of any size and offer opportunities to fit every budget either in cash or in‐kind donations. ARC appreciates the community support to realize its mission.


Major Costs Associated with ARF

  • Police and Traffic Control
  • Permits
  • Traffic Signs and Barricades
  • Trail Usage Fees ($2 per runner)
  • Aid Stations
  • Finish Line and Race Day Operations
    • Equipment and Sound
    • Facilities and Truck Rental
    • Entertainment
    • Porta-Potties
  • Advertising and Marketing
  • Website
  • Expo
  • Pasta Feed
  • Registration and Timing
  • Shirts
  • Finisher Medals
  • Awards
  • Volunteer Apparel

Partner With Us

ARF proudly recognizes all sponsors, partners and supporters. We advertise ARF through a variety of media sources and actively participate in social media. We are always open to new ideas and will be happy to discuss other ways to promote and acknowledge your sponsorship.


Sponsorship Levels

Gold:  $5,000 + (includes in‐kind)

  • Specific event element sponsorship available
  • Large logo on event and volunteer shirts.
  • Banner provided by sponsor placed along fencing at start / finish chute
  • Link on ARF website
  • Promotion on race day and pre and post race events
  • Complimentary race entries
  • Booth at Health and Fitness Expo

Silver:   $3,000+ (includes in-kind)

  • Specific event element sponsorship available
  • Logo on all event and volunteer shirts
  • Banner provided by sponsor placed along fencing at start / finish chute
  • Link on ARF website
  • Promotion on race day and pre and post race events
  • Complimentary race entries
  • Booth at Health and Fitness Expo

Bronze:   $2,000+ (includes in-kind)

  • Specific event element sponsorship available
  • Banner provided by sponsor placed along fencing at start / finish chute
  • Logo and link on ARF website, links/likes on Facebook page.
  • Promotion on race day and pre and post race events

Supporting: Under $2,000 (includes in-kind)

  • Specific event element sponsorship available
  • Llink on ARF website
  • Promotion on race day and pre and post race events

Race Sponsors -- only one event open for 2016 sponsorship!

  • Event sponsors for the following individual races have been confirmed: the 49K Ultra, marathon, half marathon, 5K, and the Kids' 2K. The Anchorage Mile is the only event currently available for 2016 race sponsorship.
