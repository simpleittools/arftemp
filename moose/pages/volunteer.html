Volunteers

The Anchorage RunFest (ARF) is an all-volunteer organization and as the race grows, so does the need for dedicated volunteers. Almost 800 volunteers fill positions such as as registration helpers, course marshals, aid station workers, finish line helpers, and many others.

Volunteers receive:

  • A ton of fun!
  • A long-sleeve technical shirt
  • Free entry to the pasta feed on Saturday, August 20.
  • The gratitude of the runners

Our complete list of volunteer positions will be available on April 1st when we open our Volunteer Hub.  Plan on returning at that time to sign up!

In the meantime, listed below are just some of the Anchorage RunFest volunteer opportunities and the rough number of folks needed to fill them. Plan on volunteering! Better yet -- get a group of friends and volunteer together! We have a ton of ways you can make a difference and have loads of fun at the same time!

  • Kids' 2K: 50
  • Bib Pick-up and Expo (set-up, run, and take-down crews): 50
  • Pasta Feed: 5
  • Volunteer Check-in: 5
  • Start/Finish Line Set-up: 20
  • Course Set-up: 10
  • Aid Station Set-up and Take-down: 20
  • Gear Check: 8
  • Bike Leaders and Sweepers: 20
  • Course Flaggers: 20
  • Ham Radio Operators: 15
  • Medal Distributers: 15
  • Awards Distributers: 3
  • Finish Line Food: 15
  • Start/Finish Line Take-down: 10
