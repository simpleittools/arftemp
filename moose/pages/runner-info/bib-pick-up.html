Bib Pickup

Bib Pickup, Last Chance Registration, Expo



Location and Date

Location: Dena'ina Center, 600 West Seventh Avenue
Date: Friday, August 19, 2016 from 3 to 7 PM. and Saturday, August 20, 2016 from 9 AM to 4 PM.


Last Chance Registration

Friday and Saturday at the Expo offer the last opportunity for registration. For these late registration fees and rules at the Bib-Pickup/Expo, see our Registration page.


Event Change

You may change events during bib pickup at the solutions table.

There are no bib pickups or race changes on Sunday, August 21, 2016.


Shirts

We do our best to get you the size you need but we can’t guarantee sizes because shirts must be ordered well in advance of our receivng all race entries. We will have a covered area on Sunday where shirts can be exchanged with our remaining inventory. This will enable all participants the best opportunity to pick up their ordered shirt size at bib pickup before exchanges occur.


Expo

Once you have all your necessary race gear, hang out and visit our Expo vendors or learn something new at our Health and Fitness Expo free clinics!
