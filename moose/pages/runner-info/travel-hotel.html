Anchorage Trolley Tours

Make the most of your stay in Anchorage by hopping aboard the Anchorage City Trolley. Visit historic spots, watch float planes depart from Lake Hood, view the breath-taking mountains, and don’t forget the camera for the many photo opportunities. Learn about Anchorage’s past and present with tips on the best places for shopping, dining, and entertainment.

Anchorage Weather and Climate

Average low/high temperature in August: 50/64 degrees F. Average precipitation in August: 2.89 inches. August 21, 2016 (race day!): Sunrise at 6:25 AM; Sunset at 9:37 PM; day length will be 15 hours 12 minutes.




Wildlife

Moose and bears live in the Anchorage bowl and surrounding mountains. They are powerful, wild animals, and should never be approached. To see them safely, visit the Alaska Zoo in Anchorage or the Alaska Wildlife Conservation Center in Portage. That said, wildlife watching is a favorite Alaskan activity, and besides the large mammals, the region is home to all kinds of birds and smaller critters. Keep your eyes open and have your camera ready!
