Race Day Amenities and Support

Pre-Race Activities

The following activities were offered in 2015. We will confirm activities for 2016 when we are closer to race day.

  • Yoga by Divine Intervention Design:  fun, upbeat and encouraging sessions
  • United Physical Therapy:  free taping for runners.

Aid Stations

All aid stations will have water and Gatorade®. There will also be water and Gatorade® at the ultramarathon turnaround on Kincaid hill. The event does not provide food at any of the aid stations, however. This is a "BYOG" event, so be sure to pack some Gu (or another energy gel of your choice) with you if needed. Aid stations are located approximately every 2 miles. For exact locations, please see the ARF All Races Combo Map.


Medical Support

Two medical tents will be available to runners, one at the start/finish and one at Westchester Lagoon. Medical personnel will patrol the entire course and provide medical support as well as transportation for injured runners.

Race Tip: Write your full name, age and emergency contact person and phone number on the back of your bib. It’s also a good idea to include any drug allergies or relevant medical conditions. This is vital information for medical personnel if you should become disoriented or incapacitated.


Finish Line Food

Racers' appetites were well provided for at the 2015 finish.

  • Franz Bakery: grilled cheese sandwiches
  • Great Harvest Bread Company: thick slices of freshly baked bread
  • Clear Alaskan Glacial: bottled water, made in Alaska
  • Nature's Bakery: fantastic fig bars
  • Alaska Chip Company: all natural kettle chips

Massages

Post-race massages were offered by the following in 2015:

  • Captain Cook Athletic Club - Chair Massages
  • Natural Health Center
  • Tuina Sports Massage
  • United Physical Therapy

In 2015 REI set up a yoga / stretching / comfort station at the finish line also.


Beer Garden

Broken Tooth Brewing will provide a beer garden in Town Square Park exclusively for runners 21 and over. Those planning to partake in this well-deserved post-race treat must have a valid ID card on their person as well as their race bib. No one, no matter how old you are, will be admitted to the beer garden without proper ID.   


Post Race Showers

The Hotel Captain Cook has invited runners to shower at the athletic club after the race. The club is located on the lower level of the Hotel, just show your bib at the desk. 939 W. 5th Avenue.
