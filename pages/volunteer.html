Volunteers

The Anchorage RunFest is an all-volunteer organization and as the race grows, so does the need for dedicated volunteers. Almost 800 volunteers fill positions such as as registration helpers, course marshals, aid station workers, finish line helpers, and many others.

Volunteers receive:

  • A ton of fun!
  • A commemorative RunFest volunteer shirt
  • The gratitude of runners


How You Can Help

You can volunteer! Better yet -- get a group of friends and volunteer together! We have a ton of ways you can make a difference and have loads of fun at the same time!

A link to the Volunteer Hub will be posted once it is open in the spring.

In the meantime, you may contact our Volunteer Coordinator directly.
