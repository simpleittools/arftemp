2021 Course Entertainment

Our list of entertainers fills up fast. Are you interested in donating your entertainment talent to encourage our runners on their quests? We look for all kinds of talent: singers, instrumentalists, bell ringers, drummers, boom box operators, etc. We're all ears! If this sounds like fun, email us and offer your unique talents!
