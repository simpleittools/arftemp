Registration

2020 Registrants

2020 Deferred Registrants. If you deferred your 2020 RunFest entry you do not need to re-register but you do need to claim your deferral on the RunSignUp web site.


2021 Online Registration

Online registration for the 2021 49K ultra, marathon, marathon walk, marathon relay, half marathon, 5K, The Anchorage Mile, and Kids' 2K is now open.

REGISTER NOW

Early registration fees will apply through December 31, 2020. Fees will increase thereafter at four intervals. Last chance, in-person registration will be on August 20 and 21 at bib pickup. Late fees will apply. Last chance registration for The Anchorage Mile will be Friday, August 20, 2021.

There is no race day registration or bib pickup. Participants may not switch events after registration closes at 4 PM on Saturday, August 21. There are no refunds. There are no transfers of bibs.

Please carefully review our refund and transfer policies below.

Engraving for Finisher Medals

If you would like to order a small metal plate, engraved with your name and finish time, to attach to the back of your Finisher's Medal, you may do so online when you register. To see what this looks like, or to order one after your event, visit iTAB's RunFest page.


Refund and Transfer Policies

All entry fees are non-refundable and non-transferable—no exceptions. You may not give, sell, or exchange your entry or bib to another person. Entry fees may not be rolled over to another year. This policy applies to all entrants and is in effect no matter what the reason for being unable to participate.


Event Changes

If you decide to change your event after your have registered, please login to RunSignUp to make the change. There is a $5.00 fee for any event change and there are additional fees for upgrades.


Bib Pickup

Bib pickup will be held on Friday, August 20, from 3:00 PM to 7:00 PM and again on Saturday, August 21, from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM at a location to be determined. There is no bib pickup on race day. Read more about bib pickup here.


Safety Rules

PLEASE NOTE: As a safety precaution for our runners, ABSOLUTELY NO strollers, skateboards, roller blades, or pets (on or off leash) will be allowed on the race course. If you are on any part of the course with any of these you will be automatically disqualified.


Run For Good

Anchorage RunFest partners with several charity organizations to allow our runners the opportunity to raise funds for these not-for-profit organizations. See our Run For Good page for a list and description of these groups.


Live Athlete Updates

Do you want to follow the progress of friends who are racing ? You can receive updates on athletes as they cross points on the course and have them sent to Facebook, Twitter, or mobile phone. The sign up link will be posted as soon as it is available.


Pasta Feed

The pasta feed will be held Saturday, August 21 from 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM at a location to be determined. Tickets are $14 per person, and may be purchased during the online registration process, or in person at bib pickup. We will have more information as race day approaches.
