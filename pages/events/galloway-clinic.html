Jeff Galloway Running School

Jeff, and his running partner wife Barbara, were scheduled to return for their 11th trip to Alaska in 2020 and Jeff was planning on offering his three hour running school.  We have not determined if Jeff will be returning in 2021.

Jeff has helped over 200,000 runners reach their goals, and wants to help you. Jeff's school offers a unique system of elements that has reduced injury rate down to almost zero, with a very high rate of success. He gives you tools to manage your fatigue, with a very accurate prediction of your current fitness level and race times, and lots of practical tips that can make running more enjoyable. Read more about Jeff's running schools.
