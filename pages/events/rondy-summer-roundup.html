Rondy Summer Roundup



Friday, August 18, 2018

Location:  Egan Civic and Convention Center, Explorer Hall, 555 West Fifth Avenue

Event: Country Western Dance

Tickets:  $25/person; $40/couple

Time:  6:00 - 10:30 PM



The Event

The 2018 Rondy Summer Roundup will feature the Ken Peltier Band, Alaska's premier country band.

Wear your country attire and take part in a night of great dancing and summertime fun!

Featuring:

  • Two-Step Lessons & Contest
  • Great Prizes

This is the official party for Anchorage RunFest and a fundraiser for Anchorage's Fur Rondy.



Purchasing Tickets

Tickets may be purchased online or at the Rondy Shop (corner of 4th Avenue and D Street).



NOTE:

Attendees must be 21 years of age or older to attend. IDs will be checked at the door.
